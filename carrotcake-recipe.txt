Ingredients:
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cup corn or sunflower oil
- 4 eggs, well beaten
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 cups grated carrots

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to moderate (175°C / 350°F). Grease a tube pan well.
2. In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and oil. Add the eggs and beat 
for a few minutes. Sift together the flour, cinnamon, baking soda and salt. 
Add to the sugar mixture. Mix the carrots. Pour the batter into the tube pan 
and bake for about 55 minutes. When cool, cover with Cream Cheese Frosting.
